Limit Cut Position Guide

Spread to the edge — use True or Relative north on the map

True North

  • A–D = circle waymarks (diamond). 1–4 = square waymarks (inner square).
  • Players stand on the arena wall, aligned from the outer edge of each square marker.
  • Start at the waymark opposite the AoE origin.
  • Assign 1→8 around the wall in the direction opposite the dash rotation.
  • Adjacent pairs (1+2, 3+4, …) share a square marker edge.
  • Relative north rotates the map so the AoE destination (opposite origin) is at the top.
