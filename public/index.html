Knowing our perceptions.

Enhanced Audition & Equilibrioception | Blue studies of Chiroptera native to the Shroud concluded as the most worthy candidate. Investigation into their neuroanatomy returned aetheric properties to enhance both auditory and vestibular abilities in the user. Likely the most outwardly used of the senses, auditory enhancements and echo capabilities allow for detailed mental mappings of objects, shapes and people, even for the amaurotic. The enhancements incur weaknesses to loud noises and vibrations.

Know thyself, all moves and all vibrates.

Enhanced Olfaction | The natural case study was the La Noscean Coeurl, their predatory instincts revealed aetheric capabilities concentrated to enhance the user's olfactory. Scent has often been utilized by predators as tracking mechanisms, the enhancement of such allows the user to easily identify familiar locations, objects and persons of interest.

Know thyself, see the world through different eyes.

Enhanced Gustation | Observation found that the Buffalo of La Noscea harbored the most advanced gustatory capabilities. Whilst not particularly as useful as many other senses, it was a valuable baseline to explore and surpass humanoid natural limitations.

Know thyself, reconcile all paradoxes.

Enhanced Tactition & Nociception | The Shroud's Diremite proved to be the apex choice for risk-reward in terms of tactition. Their ability to sense even the faintest of vibrations proved a double edge sword, that the caster might be aware of their surroundings doubly, but too may doubly enjoy the pain of a strike.

Know thyself, change manifests in all.

Enhanced Thermoception & Proprioception | Kinesthesia is perhaps the most overlooked yet paramount sense; with lack of visual feedback, the study of the Shroud's Chigoe and domestic Mameshiba demonstrated their affinity to such practice commonly regarded as a 'sixth sense.' This bodily awareness allows the user to control various aspects of their soma with minute detail, including sensibility to heat and cold from very significant distances compared to what might be considered natural.

Know thyself, and become a God amongst men.
